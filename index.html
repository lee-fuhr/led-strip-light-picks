How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own timed brightness tests or stick decibel meters on power supplies. Instead, we dig into long-term owner feedback from forums, verified purchasers, and published hands-on reviews from trusted tech outlets. We look for patterns: which strips peel off after a month, which apps crash regularly, and which models actually maintain color accuracy over years of use. We also consider reputation, brands like Philips Hue have decades of lighting expertise, while Govee has earned loyalty through consistent improvements. Price is factored in, but never in isolation; a cheap strip that fails in six months is no bargain. Our picks are the ones that real people report still loving a year later.
